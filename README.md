# Naptune Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Naptune does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All sleep, feeding, and baby information you record is stored locally on your device only. We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party services.

You can delete all your data at any time by:
- Uninstalling the app, or
- Using the "Reset all data" option in Settings

## Children's Privacy
This app is intended for parents tracking their baby's sleep. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
